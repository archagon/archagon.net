Composer's Sketchpad (2016)

Paint Music with Your Fingers

Platform: iOS (iPad)
Price: $3.99
Source Code: Eventually

Composer's Sketchpad is a new take on music notation that aims to offer a human-friendly alternative to staff music and notation software. Instead of scratching out melodies one note at a time, you can draw musical notes directly onto a pannable and zoomable canvas. Notes can start at any point and bend to any pitch along the way, allowing you to create guitar solos, complex rhythms, and other elements that have traditionally eluded musical notation.
