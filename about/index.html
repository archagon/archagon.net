About

Hello! My name is Alexei Baboulevitch, also known as Archagon. I’m a perpetually traveling software developer working on various apps and games.

This is my work-in-progress Jekyll blog, eventually meant to supplant both my Squarespace blog (http://www.archagon.net) and my programming blog (http://www.abstractrose.net, presently MIA). At the moment it’s under heavy construction, hence unfinished appearance and the beta-blog subdomain. But that doesn’t mean it’s not open to readership!

Once I finish the initial design and port all my old posts over, I’ll change the base URL to just archagon.net and redirect the beta-blog subdomain there. This hopefully means that all the beta permalinks will continue to work in perpetuity.
